Episodes About

In the Dark is a podcast tracing Sandra Birchmore's tragic case while uncovering bigger stories of injustice, institutional failure, and silence. Each episode boils down to having impact on powerful stories through accountability, reputations, institutional loyalty, and the cost of speaking truth to power. With a mix of investigative journalism and personal accounts, we hope to turn the conventions on their head that allow abuse to perpetuate and give voice to the unheard silenced by corruption and fear. Join us as we shed light on the insidious forces that collude to keep society ignorant.
